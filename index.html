Interesting Facts about Camels

  1. Camels actually do not store water in their hump, but fat!
  2. Camels are also known as ships of the desert.
  3. Camels can survive for up to four weeks without water.

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are reowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears were invented simultaneously by toymakers Morris Michtom and Richard Steiff.
  • A teddy bear once sold for $182,000.
  • The teddy bear was invented in 1902.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  1. The most famous species of pandas, the great panda, is endangered.
  2. The restaurant, Panda Express, is named after the great panda.
  3. An adult male red panda is only 1.9 feet. Compared to the great panda, that's small!

Interesting Facts about Flying Cicadas

  • Cicadas can suvive a huge fall as babies.
  • Cicadas are about the size of a grain of rice as a baby.
